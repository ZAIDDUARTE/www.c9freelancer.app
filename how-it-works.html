Joining C9Freelancer is literally as easy as 1, 2, 3.

1. Sign Up for a Free Account

Create a professional account using the built-in form. Include as much professional details as you want such as employment history, relevant experiences, certifications, and relevant skills.

We recommend that you include as many relevant skills and experiences in your profile to make it more attractive to potential employers. Moreover, constantly updating your profile with new information helps a lot. The more substantial your profile is, the better your chances of getting hired.

2. Search and Apply for Jobs

As soon as your account is created, you can start using our search engine to look for available job opportunities.

Once you see a job posting that you believe is a perfect fit for you, you can go ahead and apply for it. You can submit as many applications as you want for as many positions as you believe you can fill.

3. Get Hired

As soon as someone replies to your application, go ahead and close that deal.

Or, if your profile catches the attention of a potential employer, you'll be contacted directly. You can then work out the details of your employment with them.

If you want to only work on a freelance basis and use the platform to get as many clients as possible, you can do so. If you're looking for a full-time role, you can do that as well. You have full control of your employment details.
